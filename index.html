Welcome

This is the web site of Mahbubul Majumder. I am an Assistant Professor of statistics in the department of Mathematics, University of Nebraska at Omaha (UNO). My academic training is in statistics with specialization in data visualization and visual statistical inference. Before getting to the academic world I had the opportunity to work with some industries dealing with data and creating data products. This motivated me to work for developing a data science program at UNO.

Education

  • PhD Statistics, Iowa State University, Ames, IA, 2013
  • MA Statistics, Ball State University, Muncie, IN, 2007
  • MSc Statistics, University of Dhaka, Bangladesh, 1998
  • BSc Statistics, University of Dhaka, Bangladesh, 1996

Research Interest

  • Visual statistical inference
  • Exploratory data visualization and quantification
  • Statistical modeling
  • Data Science and big data problems
