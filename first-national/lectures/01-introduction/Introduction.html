Data Science is not statistics

  • Well, Cleveland's article says some of it
  • Does any of these get a focus in Statistics?
    • data product (vs making decision based on data)
    • methods of data cleaning (vs cleaned data is assumed)
    • data technology (vs its the job of computer scientists)
  • In statistics, data should be collected based on planned analysis methods.
    • Lots of data are now generated without having any statistical planning, but they can still provide deliverable data products
      • Google search, twitter messages, Facebook pages

Definition: Data Science

  • Arts and Science of transforming data
  • Data products

What is data product?

  • A data dependent product that has some commercial value
    • Could be another data set summarizing the original bigger data set
    • An online tool to visualize or explore important information
    • Mobile app
    • Could be a report based on data




- Examples - Kelley Blue Book (KBB) website - Netflix movie recommendation system - Your credit score

Data science components

Donoho classifies the activities of Greater Data Science into 6 divisions -

  • Data Exploration and Preparation
  • Data Representation and Transformation
  • Computing with Data
  • Data Modeling
  • Data Visualization and Presentation
  • Science about Data Science
